Synopsis

In a world where alchemy dictates the hierarchy of power, young Elian discovers an ancient manuscript that defies the established laws of equivalent exchange. Cast out from the prestigious Royal Academy, he must navigate the treacherous borderlands known as the Horizon.

Armed with forbidden knowledge and an unbreakable will, Elian forges alliances with outcasts, rebels, and forgotten spirits — each carrying their own secrets. As political factions close in and ancient powers stir, the line between salvation and destruction blurs with every transmutation.

The Horizon is not just a place — it is a test. And Elian is about to discover that some equations were never meant to be balanced.
